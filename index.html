About Us

Our organization is professionally managed through well qualified and experienced personnel in all areas including finance and administration. This has led to an efficient and effective working of us.

M/s. Rolex Realty fundamental business strategy is to build long-term client relationships. With presence of more than 8 years in the industry, we have attracted and retained clients by providing superior customer values by quality work and timeliness.

M/s. Rolex Realty was founded by Shri Bhagwati H. Jain, a small businessman with big vision that transformed us into reputed Builder & Developer Company in Mumbai.

For over a 8 long Years of experience M/s. Rolex Realty is now a professionally managed company which has successfully Completed many projects through our various group companies in Mumbai.
